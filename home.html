About

Hi! I’m Samyuktha, a passionate self-taught artist.

My journey into the world of art began at the age of 15, fueled by curiosity, observation, and an ever growing love for creativity.
With no formal training, I’ve shaped my artistic voice through consistent, self-paced practice learning through trial, sketchbooks, and inspiration from the world around me. Art has become my way to express emotions, capture moments, and explore different mediums.
This portfolio is a collection of my personal artworks, each piece holding a story, a spark, or a step forward in my creative growth.
Thank you for visiting and taking the time to see the world through my eyes.
